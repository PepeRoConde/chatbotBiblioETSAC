development strategy
1.  A high-performing university:
advancing scientific excellence by supporting a comprehensive range of cutting-edge research fields and fostering interdisciplinarity at multiple levels;
delivering state-of-the-art educational programmes by encouraging innovative pedagogy, developing interdisciplinary curricula, and empowering students in their professional development;
enhancing the efficiency of administrative support and strengthening governance capacities through the development of adequate tools.
2. An influential university:
pursuing an international strategy centred on strong strategic partnerships (such as the Circle U. European Alliance), reinforcing its international status through active involvement in European higher education and research initiatives, expanding internationalisation activities (abroad and at home), and cultivating strategic partnerships with a select number of world-class institutions worldwide;
building a unique innovation ecosystem in Europe, bringing together national research organisations, laboratories of excellence, industrial partners, experimental platforms, Company for Accelerating Technology Transfer (SATT) and more;
implementing a dedicated alumni policy.
3. A welcoming university:
offering student experience that fosters both success and personal fulfilment, combining academic and extracurricular opportunities to create a vibrant campus life;
promoting sustainable development, equality, diversity, and inclusion, while acting as a responsible employer.
