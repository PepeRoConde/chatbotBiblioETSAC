Governance bodies and academic commissions
The integral regulations of the Faculty of Nursing and Podiatry contain within Title II the description, composition, choice and information linked to the bodies of the center.
In addition, Chapter IV in Article 31 provides that the purpose of the centre's commissions will be linked to being bodies dependent on the Faculty Board, for the best performance of the functions, which will be:
- Permanent for matters entrusted by the Faculty Board within the framework of the competences, and will have the same duration as this.
- Non-permanent are created and designated by the Faculty Board for the realization of concrete work, and the duration of these will be consistent with the eventuality.
