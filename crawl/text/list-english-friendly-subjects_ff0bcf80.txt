LIST OF ENGLISH FRIENDLY SUBJECTS
LIST OF ENGLISH FRIENDLY SUBJECTS
Es
Gal
As part of the Annual Planning Procedure for English Friendly Courses, the list of subjects and teaching staff offering courses in this modality at the Faculty of Communication Sciences for the upcoming academic year is now available.
The proposal, which was approved on 25 June 2025 by the Faculty Board, reflects the Faculty’s commitment to internationalization and to promoting the use of English as a support language in teaching.
The list of subjects and teaching staff involved is as follows:
Bachelor’s Degree in Audiovisual Communication
3º curso. Infography 1 (code 616G01024): Francisco Javier Taibo Pena
4º curso. 3D Interaction (code 616G01044): Luis Antonio Hernández Ibáñez
Bachelor’s Degree in Digital Creation, Animation and Video Games
1º curso. Modelling 1 (code 616G02015): Francisco Javier Taibo Pena
4º curso. Business and Entrepreneurship (code 616G02046): Ariadna Monje Amor
4º curso. Shading (code 616G02027): José Antonio Iglesias Guitián
4º curso. Advanced Rendering Techniques (code 616G02024): José Antonio Iglesias Guitián
