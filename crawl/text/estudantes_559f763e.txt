Students
The University of A Coruña guarantees the equal rights and responsibilities of all its students. It also guarantees that no distinction will be made between them except in relation to their chosen course of study, and that no discrimination will be permitted for reasons of birth, race, gender, language, financial capacity, disability, sexual orientation or opinion, or on any other personal, social, religious and/or political grounds.
The University of A Coruña also guarantees the right to freedom of speech of all its students, in accordance with Article 16 of the Spanish Constitution and the implementing legislation.
