Study
The courses offered by the university are in a constant state of change and development to meet the needs of society. Alongside traditional single majors, the university now offers double degree and international study programmes, degrees taught through English, and a range of Master’s, PhD and self-accredited postgraduate programmes, as well as the University of Lifelong Learning. You can consult our studies in the Chatbot UDC .

Self-accredited postgraduate programmes
The UDC Office of Self-Accredited Postgraduate Programmes is the office responsible for the programming, coordination, supervision and communication of UDC self-accredited postgraduate programmes. It is also responsible for ensuring that our programmes meet the postgraduate learning needs and standards of prospective students.
University of Lifelong Learning
The University of Lifelong Learning is a university study programme aimed at people over the age of 50 who are no longer working but would like to engage in a modern course of study that feeds their intellectual curiosity and cultural awareness.

Students
The University of A Coruña guarantees the equal rights and responsibilities of all its students. It also guarantees that no distinction will be made between them except in relation to their chosen course of study, and that no discrimination will be permitted for reasons of birth, race, gender, language, financial capacity, disability, sexual orientation or opinion, or on any other personal, social, religious and/or political grounds.
The University of A Coruña also guarantees the right to freedom of speech of all its students, in accordance with Article 16 of the Spanish Constitution and the implementing legislation.
