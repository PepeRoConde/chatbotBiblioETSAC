Services in the FIC.
Cafetería
The FIC cafeteria is on the
1st floor. Its timetableis from
Monday to Friday from 8:00 
a.m. to 9:00 p.m. At noon it

In the 3rd floor of the FIC 
you will find the
reprography service. Open

✚SAPE
The Student Advice and 
Promotion Service (SAPE) is the
reference informationservice for
students at the UDC. It informs on
a wide range of aspects of 
university life: access to studies, 
regulations, awards, scholarships
and grants, UDC services, 
transportation, accommodation, 
etc. It also has specific work
guidance and entrepreneurship
staff to advise students and 
recent graduates. Youcan find it
in the glass buildingin front of 
the exit of the FIC from Floor 0.
✚
ADI
The University Unit for Diversity
Assistance (ADI) facilitates and 
promotes the full integration of 
students with some type of 
disability.

SNL
The Linguistic Normalization

various promotional activities in 
Galician.
