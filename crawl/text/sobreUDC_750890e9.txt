The University of A Coruña was established under the Galician University Planning Act 11/1989 (20 July).
The university is divided into two campuses, located in the cities of A Coruña and Ferrol .

Government
University government is divided into two types of bodies:
Councils and committees: Social Committee, University Council, Faculty, school and faculty boards, department and university institute committees.
Offices: President, Vice-Presidents, Registrar, University Manager, Deans, Vice-deans and Faculty Secretaries, Heads of School, Deputy Heads of School and School Secretaries, Heads of Department, Deputy Heads of Department and Department Secretaries, Heads of University Institute, Deputy Heads of University Institute and University Institute Secretaries.
