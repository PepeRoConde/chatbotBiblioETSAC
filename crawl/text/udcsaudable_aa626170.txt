UDC Saudable, at the University of A Coruña, is a service under the Vice-Rectorate of the Ferrol Campus and Social Responsibility. Its mission is to participate in the coordination and implementation of various healthy living initiatives within the university community, with a strong social projection. These initiatives include physical and sports activities, occupational activity — daily routines, reading, and leisure time — healthy eating, psychosocial well-being, sound and rest, sexual health, actions against the use and abuse of alcohol, tobacco, drugs, and other addictions, the healthy inclusion of people with diversity and frailty (such as children, older adults, and those with illnesses), pedagogical guidance, among other aspects; all in cross-cutting collaborative harmony with sustainable development at the UDC.

POR QUE PROM OCION DA SAUDE E UNIVERSIDADE?

A Promocién de Satide é AUTOCUIDARSE PARA MELLORAR A SAUDE e
tamén basease en actuar CONXUNTAMENTE COA POBOACION para
potenciar a VIDA SAUDABLE das persoas (Declaracién de Otawa, 1986)

A Promocién de Satide é “o proceso que permite 4s persoas INCREMENTAR O
CONTROL SOBRE A SUA SAUDE, abarcando unha ampla gama de
intervencidns sociais e ambientais destinadas a BENEFICIAR E PROTEXER A
SAUDE E A CALIDADE DE VIDA INDIVIDUAIS” (01S, 2076)
