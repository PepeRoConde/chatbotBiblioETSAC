Welcome to the Faculty of Tourism at the University of A Coruña
Welcome to the Faculty of Tourism at the University of A Coruña – a young, dynamic institution located in one of Spain’s most vibrant coastal cities. As an international student, you’ll find A Coruña a welcoming and lively place where tourism plays a central role in the city’s identity and economy. Our faculty reflects this spirit, offering a forward-thinking academic environment that combines innovative teaching with strong local and global connections in the field of tourism.
Check out our website here: https://udc.gal/turismo and follow us on Instagram (@turismo_udc)
Whether you're considering a short exchange or a full degree, we invite you to explore the opportunities available here and become part of our growing international community. If you would like more information, please contact us using the details below.
If you are an international student interested in completing a period of study with us (as part of the Erasmus programme or a bilateral agreement), the international Relations and Mobility Office will also provide you with the information you need and assist with the application procedure.
