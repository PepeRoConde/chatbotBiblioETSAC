Master degrees
Our academic offer also includes seven master degrees:
Master in Informatics Engineering ( https://www.fic.udc.es/gl/muei )
Master in Artificial Intellgence ( https://estudos.udc.es/en/study/start/4544V01 )
Master in Bioinformatics for Health Sciences ( http://www.master.bioinformatica.fic.udc.es/ )
Master in Cybersecurity ( http://www.munics.es/ )
Master in High Performance Computing ( http://masterhpc.es /)
Master in Computer Vision ( http://www.imcv.eu/ )
Master in Industrial Mathematics ( http://m2i.es/ )
Master in Statistical Techniques ( http://eio.usc.es/pub/mte/index.php/gl )
All Master degrees are offered in Spanish/Galician. In addition, some subjects in the Master in High Performance Computing are offered in English.
Language requirements
There is no strict language requirement if you plan to enroll in courses offered in Spanish. However, a B1 level or superior is highly recommended.
If you plan to enroll in courses offered in English, a level equivalent to CEFR B1 or superior is required.
More information and contact
If you have any question about academic aspects of our School, you can contact the mobility coordinator (Diego Andrade) at erasmus.fic@udc.es .
The web page of the International Relations Office provides more information regarding other aspects of studying at University of A Coruña: https://www.udc.es/ori
