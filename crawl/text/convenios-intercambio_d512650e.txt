Exchange programmes
The Civil Engineering School has a wide and fluid exchange program with some 50 universities all around the world, thanks to the Erasmus+ program and the bilateral agreements between the UDC and other institutions. Students can, if they wish, carry out exchanges at both the degree and the master, including the Final Projects at certain institutions. The international exchanges are especially advised for the third year of the degree. The double degree with the prestigious Politecnico di Milano is also encouraged for the master students.
Diego Carro Coordinator of mobility
