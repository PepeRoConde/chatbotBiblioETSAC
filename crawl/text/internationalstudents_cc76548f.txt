The Faculty of Philology offers three bachelor degrees: English: Linguistic and Literary Studies; Galician and Portuguese: Linguistic and Literary Studies; Spanish: Linguistic and Literary Studies. We also offer several master’s degrees and PhD programs.
Our Faculty and our University already boasts of a thriving culture in Erasmus exchange programs where international students and academic personnel enjoy an international experience in a context where integration comes naturally.
