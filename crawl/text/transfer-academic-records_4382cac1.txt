The Universidade da Coruña has opened the application period for admission by transfer from other universities or from official Spanish and foreign university studies for the 2025/2026 academic year.
Interested applicants may submit their request from July 1 to July 10, 2025.
The required documentation must be submitted to the Integrated Academic Management Unit (UXAI Sociology / Communication Sciences) or sent by email to uxai.socom@udc.es within the indicated period.
