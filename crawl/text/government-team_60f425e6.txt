Dean The dean of the faculty is the individual governing body of the centre. This figure is responsible for the direction of the centre. He presides and coordinates its collegiate bodies, and is responsible for putting their decisions into practice. He must be one of the professors or permanent lecturers from the centre, and is elected by the committee of the centre, and is nominated by the rector. This post holds a six-year mandate.
Vicedeans The vicedeans direct and co-ordinate the specific area which is assigned to them by the dean.
Vicedean for Student, Internship and Internationalization.. Mercedes Marina López Garrido.
Vicedean for Quality, Infrastructure and Teachin Staff. Guillermo Franganillo Parrado.
Secretary of faculty The secretary is the validating official for the acts and agreements decided on by the governing and administrative bodies of the centre.
Secretary of the Dean's Office The Secretary of the Dean's Office coordinates and administers the Dean's team's tasks. They manage documentation, minutes and internal communications, and collaborate with the Dean on academic and organisational activities.
