CHANGE OF AVC DEGREE
CHANGE OF AVC DEGREE
Es
Gal
The UDC's Bachelor's Degree in Audiovisual Communication is being modified to significantly change its educational structure. This update reflects our commitment to providing education that adapts to the current needs of the sector and emerging professional profiles. Our aim is to continue improving the quality of education that our students receive.
The new curriculum seeks to reinforce the practical dimension, update content and encourage specialisation, thus responding to the demands of the labour market and society. It demonstrates our firm commitment to training professionals with a comprehensive and versatile vision of the audiovisual field.
The new plan will be implemented progressively according to the following schedule:
2025-2026 academic year: 1st year 2026-2027 academic year: 1st and 2nd years 2027-2028 academic year: 1st, 2nd, and 3rd years 2028-2029 academic year: 1st, 2nd, 3rd, and 4th years
The Faculty of Communication Sciences would like to thank the students for their trust, and we will continue to work hard to provide a leading degree programme in the field of audiovisual studies.
