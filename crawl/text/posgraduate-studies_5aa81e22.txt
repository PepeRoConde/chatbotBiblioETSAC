The University of Coruña offers post-graduate studies for graduates who wish to attain the level of technical knowledge needed to complete their education, and who are keen to improve and update their skill set.
The courses are focused on providing a current response to the growing need for specialized training of both new graduates and experienced professionals.
Some of the courses include the opportunity to participate in internships with businesses and institutions.
Detailed information can be found on this website about these and other academic opportunities
