Nanoscience and nanotechnology
https://estudos.udc.es/en/study/start/610G04V01
The Degree in Nanoscience and Nanotechnology is a comprehensive, multidisciplinary and transversal program to train professionals and entrepreneurs in these sectors considered vital in the future. In the current context, and in the medium term, Nanoscience and Nanotechnology will consolidate as an industrial sector that will provide the consumer with a large number of technology-based products. The enormous range of possibilities that this new, highly technical industrial sector will open, makes it essential to have specialists in this field, which will be an essential pillar of the new industry, the competitiveness of a country and all aspects of the knowledge society.
In the academic year 2020-2021, the first year is implemented.
