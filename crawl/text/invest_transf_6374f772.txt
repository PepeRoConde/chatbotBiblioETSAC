New Models for Fluid-Thin Structure Interaction with Applications
The project New Models for Fluids-Thin Structure Interaction with Applications focuses on studying the interaction between fluids and thin structures, including contact scenarios. It develops mathematical and numerical models, along with simulation algorithms, to advance the understanding and practical application of these phenomena in various scientific and industrial fields.
