University Master’s Degree in Banking and Finance (MUBF)
Title accredited by the CNMV and the Bank of Spain to inform and advise on investment products and real estate credit contracts
The objective of this master's degree is for students to acquire the knowledge and skills necessary to develop professionally in the financial management of companies, banking and financial intermediation, and professional activities related to financial products and/or risks.

ACCESS PROFILE
University degree
The master’s degree is taught in Spanish and all teaching and assessment activities are carried out in that language. Students whose native language is not Spanish must consider whether they have or achieve sufficient linguistic competence to follow the classes and carry out all the academic activities that the completion of the Master’s degree implies in a completely normal manner. In addition, the use of electronic devices will not be allowed in exams and tests.
COURSE OBJECTIVE
To carry out professional tasks in the area of management and/or financial management of companies.
To provide financial intermediation services, either independently or within a financial institution.
To act as an independent professional providing advisory services or financial management.
